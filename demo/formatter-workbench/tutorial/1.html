Welcome

This workbench is for learning about Hypergrid cell formatting using localizers and editing using cell editors.

You can also use it for actual development and testing of such objects, cutting-and-pasting the final results to your own app.

For your convenience, the app persists your work between sessions to local storage (which does not expire).
